DATA SCIENCE WORKSHOP

"DATA SCIENCE WORKSHOP"

Associate Partner upGrad

The event held at the Seminar Hall of the Administration Block at Noida International University, was a highly informative and engaging session that unfolded from 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM. The event aimed to enlighten participants on the applications, trends, and future of Data Science.

"Data Science Workshop"

Unleashing the Power of Data: upGrad's Data Science Workshop at Noida International University

Dive into the dynamic world of data exploration and analysis with the "Data Science Workshop" hosted by a stellar team of five members from upGrad at Noida International University. This event brought together Chandra Prakash, Avneet Kaur, Ashish Rawat, Chaman Shagufta, and Ashok Pathak, each an expert in their respective domains, to impart invaluable insights into the realm of Data Science.

"Speaker Ms. Avneet Kaur"

With a passion for transforming data into insights, Ms. Avneet Kaur's dynamic approach captivated the audience, making the complex world of Data Science accessible and exciting.

"Mr. Chandra Prakash"

A visionary leader, Chandra Prakash is at the forefront of cutting-edge developments in Data Science, bringing his wealth of expertise to the students.

"Team upGrad"

These trailblazers, each a maestro in their own right, joined forces to deliver an engaging and enlightening workshop on Data Science.

"NIU Authorities Collaborate with upGrad: A Unified Commitment to Education"

In a noteworthy collaboration, the authorities at Noida International University (NIU) have joined forces with upGrad, a leading education platform offering PGPs and Master's programs in various fields including AI, ML, Data Science, and Digital Marketing. This collaboration signifies a shared commitment to providing high-quality education and professional development opportunities for students and professionals alike.

"Recognition of Excellence: Dr. Vimal Bibhu Honors Mr. Ashok Pathak"

In a moment of acknowledgment and appreciation, Dr. Vimal Bibhu, the (Associate Dean) of the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department, bestowed a token of appreciation upon Mr. Ashok Pathak.

"Recognition of Excellence: Dr. Arijit Tomar Honors Ms. Avneet Kaur"

HOD of SET, Dr. Arjit Tomar, expressed appreciation for the insightful session by presenting a token of appreciation to Ms. Avneet Kaur, who served as the key speaker. Ms. Kaur shared valuable knowledge and experiences, contributing to the students' understanding of Data Science concepts.

"End of Event"

Their visit wasn't just a workshop; it was a journey into the future of data exploration and problem-solving. The uoGrad team's commitment to empowering minds echoes in the halls of Noida International University, where the seeds of curiosity and innovation were sown.
