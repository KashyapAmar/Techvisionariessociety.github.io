Dr.Arjit Tomar
(HOD CSE)

Dr. Arjit Tomar holds a Ph.D., M.Tech, and B.Tech, specializing in Database Management System and Computer Network. With six years of teaching experience, he currently works at Noida International University. Previously, he served as an Assistant Professor at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaja University, Mumbai, and held positions at Bhagwant Institute Of Technology, Ghaziabad, and Bhagwant University Ajmer.
