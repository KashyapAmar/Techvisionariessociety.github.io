Inaugration of Techrise

"Inaugration of Techrise"

A journey where technology meets vision, and our collective brilliance illuminates the path to a future shaped by the hands of visionaries.

Under the visionary leadership of Dr. (Prof.) Uma Bhardwaj, the inauguration marked the formal establishment of Techrise Visionaries as a hub for technological innovation and collaboration. The club is set to become a dynamic platform for students to explore, create, and excel in the ever-evolving field of technology. The presence of our Vice-Chancellor added a significant touch to the occasion, symbolizing the university's commitment to fostering a culture of innovation and excellence. The event was a testament to our collective dedication to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and technology.

"Opening Ceremony of Club"

Dr. (Prof.) Uma Bhardwaj, with grace and vision, illuminated the inauguration of Techrise Visionaries, our esteemed technical club, fostering innovation and excellence in the School of Engineering and Technology

"Showcasing of Website"

The Club President showcased the dedicated club website to Vice-Chancellor Ma'am, seamlessly blending technology and vision, symbolizing our commitment to innovation and effective communication in our academic journey.

"Dr. Anand Kumar Shukla interacting with Club Members"

The Director of School of Engineering and Technology actively engaged with the club, asking insightful queries. Their involvement reflects a commitment to understanding and supporting the club's endeavors.

"Words from Hon'ble Vice Chancellor Ma'am and Director Sir"

Club members attentively listened to the valuable insights shared by Director Sir and Vice-Chancellor Ma'am, fostering a spirit of collaboration and learning within the dynamic environment of the club.

"End of Event"

All club members gathered for a joyous group picture, capturing the spirit of unity and shared aspirations, marking a memorable moment in the journey of Techrise Visionaries.
